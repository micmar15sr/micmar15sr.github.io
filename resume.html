About me

Currently I am a Research Fellow in Southern University of Applied Sciences and Arts of Italian Switzerland (SUPSI). My main interest are molecular dynamics simulations for proteins and bio-inspired materials. I also do research on matematical modell of hemodialysis and hyperscanning in psicology.
